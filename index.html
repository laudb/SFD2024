Software Freedom Day (SFD) is a worldwide celebration of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS).

about Linux Accra User Group

We are the oldest and the largest Linux user group in Ghana. We are a community made up of Open source and Linux hobbyists, professionals, enthusiasts, developers as well as newbies.Our members share interests in Linux and other free and open source software and projects. We meet every Saturday from 2pm to 5pm (14:00 - 17:00) at the Ghana-India Kofi Annan Centre of Excellence in ICT - Advance Information Technology Institute, is located at 2nd Avenue Ridge, Opposite the Council of State.
